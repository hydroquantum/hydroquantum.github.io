## Overview

QuantumIrrigation is an open-source framework that integrates quantum computing and deep learning for irrigation demand assessment. The project combines Variational Quantum Circuits (VQC), Quantum Long Short-Term Memory (QLSTM), and hybrid quantum-classical architectures to predict reference evapotranspiration (ETo) and soil water tension using weather and soil measurements from agricultural systems. By benchmarking quantum and hybrid models against state-of-the-art deep learning approaches, QuantumIrrigation explores the potential of quantum-enhanced methods for precision irrigation management and water-efficient agriculture.
